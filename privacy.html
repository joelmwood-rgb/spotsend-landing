Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

SpotSend ("we," "us," "our") respects the privacy of contractors who use our platform and the homeowners who interact with our messaging features. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it (very narrowly), and how you can exercise your rights regarding your information.

SMS data notice: Phone numbers, SMS opt-in status, and consent data are NOT shared with third parties for their marketing purposes. We do not sell your phone number. SMS-related information is used solely to provide the SpotSend service and is not used for promotional outreach by us or anyone else.

1. Who We Are

SpotSend is a B2B SaaS platform serving home-service contractors (concrete, roofing, and similar trades) with direct mail marketing tools and lead capture infrastructure. Our platform is operated by JWood, contact email: joelmwood@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

From Contractors using SpotSend:

  • Account information: name, email, business name, business address, phone number, billing information (processed by Stripe — we do not store full card numbers)
  • Usage data: postcards sent, addresses targeted, leads captured, calls received, settings preferences
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, session activity

From Homeowners interacting with SpotSend numbers (via contractor postcards):

  • Phone number (when calling or texting a contractor's SpotSend number)
  • SMS message content (when texting a SpotSend number — limited to the message itself)
  • Call metadata: time, duration, whether the call was answered
  • Voicemail recordings and transcripts (if Phase 2 voicemail features are enabled)

3. How We Use Information

We use information to operate the SpotSend service, specifically:

  • Generate and ship postcards on behalf of contractors
  • Forward inbound calls and SMS messages to contractors' personal phones
  • Log calls and messages so contractors can review their inbound activity
  • Process payments via Stripe
  • Provide customer support and improve the platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. SMS-Specific Data Practices

When a homeowner texts a SpotSend phone number:

  • The message and the homeowner's phone number are forwarded to the contractor who owns that SpotSend number, so the contractor can respond.
  • The message is logged in the contractor's SpotSend dashboard for their reference.
  • The homeowner's phone number and message are NOT shared with any third party for marketing purposes.
  • The homeowner's phone number is NOT added to any marketing list.
  • Homeowners can opt out of further communications by replying STOP at any time. Opt-outs are honored immediately and persistently.

5. Sharing of Information

We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With contractors: Inbound homeowner messages and call data are shared with the specific contractor whose SpotSend number was contacted, since that's the entire point of the service.
  • With service providers: We use third-party services to operate the platform: Supabase (database), Stripe (payments), Lob (postcard printing/mailing), Twilio (phone/SMS infrastructure), Cloudinary (images), Anthropic (AI for postcard copy generation), Vercel (hosting). Each receives only the data necessary to perform their function.
  • For legal reasons: If required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights, safety, or property.

We do not sell personal information. We do not share information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain account data for the life of your contractor account plus 30 days after deletion. Inbound call/SMS logs are retained as long as the contractor account is active. Voicemail recordings (where applicable) are retained for 90 days unless the contractor manually deletes them sooner.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, email joelmwood@gmail.com. We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, delete it, and opt out of any "sale" (we do not sell). Contact joelmwood@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

8. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, session management, and analytics (PostHog, Sentry). These help us understand how the platform is used and detect errors. You can disable cookies in your browser, though parts of the service may not function correctly.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security: encrypted connections (HTTPS), encrypted database storage, restricted access controls, and audit logging. No system is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

10. Children's Privacy

SpotSend is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to contractor account holders. Continued use of SpotSend after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: joelmwood@gmail.com
